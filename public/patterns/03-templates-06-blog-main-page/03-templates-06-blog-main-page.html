ABOUT THE PROJECT

The labels in the Amerine collection tell the story of the growing wine industry during a pivotal time. Right now, the only way to search Amerine’s collection of historic wine labels is to flip through the hundreds of spiral-bound notebooks housed in Special Collections at the UC Davis Library.

The Label This project allows you to help us mark up these labels to create a searchable database.

TELL US YOUR STORY

Are you a wine expert? Do you have family history that you’d like to share with us? Send us your story!

SUPPORT THE PROJECT

There’s much more we want to do with the labels in the Amerine collection. To help support our digital wine initiatives, please contact Jean Korinke, Director of Development, at jfkorinke@ucdavis.edu.
