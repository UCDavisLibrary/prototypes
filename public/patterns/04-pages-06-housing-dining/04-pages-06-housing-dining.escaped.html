undergraduate year at UC Davis, including if you are a graduate or professional student, the options expand. <a href="">Student Housing operates</a> many housing areas. There’s also <a href="">West Village</a>, the <a href="">nation’s largest planned zero net energy community</a>. <a href="">Private apartments and homes</a> are also available in our student-friendly Davis community.</p> <h3>What about a car?</h3> <div class="wysiwyg-feature-block align-right width-half"> <h3 class="wysiwyg-feature-block__title">How much does it cost for first-years on campus?</h3> <div class="wysiwyg-feature-block__body"> Learn more about our housing rates and costs for the dining commons meal plans on our <a href="http://housing.ucdavis.edu/prospective/fees_reshalls.asp">housing website’s fees page</a>. </div> </div> <p>Freshmen living on campus <a href="">aren’t allowed to bring their cars</a> or purchase a campus parking permit. But we have a great student-operated <a href="">Unitrans bus service</a> free for UC Davis undergraduate students that serves the entire city of Davis. Most students either bicycle or walk to and from campus. Check out the campus <a href="">parking program for residents</a> or <a href="">parking information for those living off campus</a>.</p> </div> </div> <div class="box-wrapper body-text"> <div class="panel-pane"> <h2 class="uc-underline">How’s the food?</h2> <p>Our <a href="">award-winning</a> UC Davis residential dining commons offer a delicious array of choices to suit all tastes, and the UC Davis campus and downtown are known for tasty fare that doesn’t bust the budget. Beyond the dining program in the residential halls, students can find eateries across campus including those at the <a href="">Silo</a>, <a href="">Coffee House</a> and <a href="">Student Community Center</a>. Or you can get a quick bite at one of our many food trucks. Often students use <a href="">Aggie Cash</a> as an easy way to pay for campus food. It’s easy: You can either load money onto a card online or pay for it at our Meal Card Office.</p><p>In addition to all of the dining options on campus, the city of Davis has a wide variety of restaurants with reasonable prices and flexible hours perfect for students. Some of them even accept Aggie Cash!</p> <div class="responsive-iframe responsive-iframe--16x9 responsive-iframe--16x9"> <iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/fXJ2rU8zPN8" width="853"
