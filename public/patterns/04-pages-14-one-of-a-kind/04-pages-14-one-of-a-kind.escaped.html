We’re a unique and energetic community built on diverse backgrounds, experiences and expertise; and we're intent on making the world a better place. These are our stories.</p> </div> </div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item mason-grid__item--width2"> <div class="responsive-iframe responsive-iframe--16x9 "> <iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/PAwB_t_iM7U" width="853" height="480" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe> </div> </div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item"> <div class="profile-highlight category-brand--sunny-grass "> <div class="profile-highlight__image"> <a href="https://youtu.be/PAwB_t_iM7U" title=""><img src="../../images/sample/one/profiles/sarah-stewart.jpg" alt="One of a Kind: Sarah Stewart" /></a> </div> <div class="profile-highlight__body"> <h3 class="profile-highlight__title category-brand__text">Sarah Stewart</h3> <ul class="profile-highlight__tags"> <li>Shockwave Scientist</li> <li>Moon Maven</li> <li>Science Fiction Fan Girl</li> </ul> <div class="profile-highlight__content"> <div class="profile-highlight__frontside"> <p>“I’ve always had a fascination with science fiction. It’s partly why I chose to study planetary sciences, along with a love of physics and a belief in the likelihood of life beyond our solar system. Sometimes, I feel like I’m starring in a science fiction film. I get to play explorer, researching how large bodies impact planets and result in the unexpected. There is a tremendous feeling of excitement when I discover something new.”</p> <span class="profile-highlight__cta category-brand__arrow category-brand__arrow--left">Details</span> </div> <div class="profile-highlight__flipside"> <h4>Title:</h4><p>Professor, <a href="http://geology.ucdavis.edu/">Earth and Planetary Sciences</a></p><h4>College:</h4><p><a href="http://ls.ucdavis.edu/mps/index.html">Letters and Science&#8212;Mathematical and Physical Sciences</a></p> <span class="profile-highlight__cta category-brand__arrow category-brand__arrow--right">Back</span> </div> </div> </div> </div> </div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item"> <div class="profile-highlight category-brand--sunny-grass "> <div class="profile-highlight__image"> <a href="https://youtu.be/BAkEBZeyEIo" title=""><img src="../../images/sample/one/profiles/edward-silva.jpg" alt="One of a Kind: Edward Silva" /></a> </div> <div class="profile-highlight__body"> <h3 class="profile-highlight__title category-brand__text">Edward Silva ’12</h3> <ul class="profile-highlight__tags"> <li>Idea Incubator</li> <li>World Hunger Challenger</li> <li>International Ambassador</li> </ul> <div class="profile-highlight__content"> <div class="profile-highlight__frontside"> <p>“Agriculture and food literally touch every single person on earth, and not many people know that it all starts with small-scale farmers. To help them support the world’s food supply, my company created Henlight, a small solar-powered light that uses smart technology to boost poultry egg production by up to 25 percent. The positive response from producers tells me we’re making a difference, and that is rather humbling.”</p> <span class="profile-highlight__cta category-brand__arrow category-brand__arrow--left">Details</span> </div> <div class="profile-highlight__flipside"> <h4>Degree:</h4><p>B.S., <a href="http://www.caes.ucdavis.edu/students/future/academics/majors/iad">International Agricultural Development</a></p><h4>Title:</h4><p>Co-Founder, <a href="http://www.henlight.com/">Henlight</a></p><h4>College:</h4><p><a href="http://www.caes.ucdavis.edu/">Agricultural and Environmental Sciences</a></p> <span class="profile-highlight__cta category-brand__arrow category-brand__arrow--right">Back</span> </div> </div> </div> </div> </div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item"> <div class="profile-highlight category-brand--sunny-grass "> <div class="profile-highlight__image"> <a href="https://youtu.be/PQ9giG6naH4" title=""><img src="../../images/sample/one/profiles/sarah-moffitt.jpg" alt="One of a Kind: Sarah Moffitt" /></a> </div> <div class="profile-highlight__body"> <h3 class="profile-highlight__title category-brand__text">Sarah Moffitt ’14</h3> <ul class="profile-highlight__tags"> <li>Science Superhero</li> <li>NPR Nut</li> <li>Women in STEM Supporter</li> </ul> <div class="profile-highlight__content"> <div class="profile-highlight__frontside"> <p>“My love for the ocean began as a kid on my grandfather’s boat, fishing for salmon and crabbing in the Pacific Northwest. Those early experiences with my ‘Papa’ laid the foundation for my career in marine science. At UC Davis, I spent my days aboard a research vessel off the Northern California coast. I examined sediment core samples from the deep sea to understand how marine ecosystems can flourish. I’m hopeful for a future where salmon are crazy abundant in the rivers where I grew up.”</p> <span class="profile-highlight__cta category-brand__arrow category-brand__arrow--left">Details</span> </div> <div class="profile-highlight__flipside"> <h4>Degree:</h4> <p>Ph.D., <a href="http://ecology.ucdavis.edu/">Ecology</a></p> <h4>Title:</h4> <p>Postdoctoral Scholar, <a href="http://ice.washington.edu/">Future of Ice Initiative, University of Washington</a></p> <h4>College:</h4> <p><a href="http://cbs.ucdavis.edu/index.html">Biological Sciences</a></p> <span class="profile-highlight__cta category-brand__arrow category-brand__arrow--right">Back</span> </div> </div> </div> </div> </div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item"> <div class="profile-highlight category-brand--sunny-grass "> <div class="profile-highlight__image"> <a href="https://youtu.be/Gw5Xr2FfIkM" title=""><img src="../../images/sample/one/profiles/dean-shreve.jpg" alt="One of a Kind: Dean Shreve '12, '15" /></a> </div> <div class="profile-highlight__body"> <h3 class="profile-highlight__title category-brand__text">Dean Shreve ‘12, ‘15</h3> <ul class="profile-highlight__tags"> <li>Lifetime Literature Lover</li> <li>Valued Veteran</li> <li>Recovering Class Clown</li> </ul> <div class="profile-highlight__content"> <div class="profile-highlight__frontside"> <p>“I was a horrible student. One day, my sixth-grade teacher got absolutely fed up with me because I was disrupting the class. Instead of detention, I had to lead the topic discussions for <em>Julie of the Wolves</em>, the book we were reading at the time. Turned out, I loved my ‘punishment.’ A book that had been dreadful for me — and several of my classmates, actually — came alive. I told my teacher that I wanted to teach English literature someday, and she agreed. For the first time in my life, she told my parents that I was an acceptable student.”</p> <span class="profile-highlight__cta category-brand__arrow category-brand__arrow--left">Details</span> </div> <div class="profile-highlight__flipside"> <h4>Degree:</h4> <p>B.A., <a href="http://english.ucdavis.edu/">English</a>; <a href="http://education.ucdavis.edu/teaching-credentialma">Cred</a></p> <h4>Title:</h4> <p>English Teacher, Sem Yeto Satellite High School</p> <h4>Professional School:</h4> <p><a href="http://education.ucdavis.edu/">School of Education</a></p> <h4>College:</h4> <p><a href="http://ls.ucdavis.edu/harcs/departments.html">Letters and Science&#8212;Humanities, Arts and Cultural Studies</a></p> <span class="profile-highlight__cta category-brand__arrow category-brand__arrow--right">Back</span> </div> </div> </div> </div> </div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item"> <div class="profile-highlight category-brand--sunny-grass "> <div class="profile-highlight__image"> <a href="https://youtu.be/C7iAisiL8Os" title=""><img src="../../images/sample/one/profiles/lisa-wolbert.jpg" alt="One of a Kind: Lisa Wolbert" /></a> </div> <div class="profile-highlight__body"> <h3 class="profile-highlight__title category-brand__text">Lisa Wolbert</h3> <ul class="profile-highlight__tags"> <li>Taiko Tempo Tuner</li> <li>Environmental Enthusiast</li> <li>Bee Lover</li> </ul> <div class="profile-highlight__content"> <div class="profile-highlight__frontside"> <p>“Playing taiko drums helps me connect with my Japanese heritage. It’s something I’ve always wanted to do, but never had a chance. I was surprised by the physical and mental strength required to create that explosive energy. When I reach the height of a performance, it’s a thrilling experience. I feel incredible.”</p> <span class="profile-highlight__cta category-brand__arrow category-brand__arrow--left">Details</span> </div> <div class="profile-highlight__flipside"> <h4>MAJOR:</h4> <p><a
