What can you do HERE?

Here at UC Davis, you will make your mark. By forging great friendships, joining clubs, playing sports and taking part in campus life, you will make the most of your time as a college student — and find out who you are. This campus is rich with traditions like Picnic Day and Homecoming, but you will also be part of our biggest Aggie tradition — doing things your way while transforming our university and making the world a better place. You’ll pick up life skills along the way, like leadership. And you’ll find UC Davis is one of a kind, like you.

Cheer your heart out

Our 23 Division I athletics teams love your support. Join Aggie Pack, the nation’s largest college spirit group, and show your Aggie spirit.

Socialize

So many choices for meet ups: the Quad, the sports fields, the Silo, the arboretum, downtown, the CoHo … where will you start?

Get moving

Day and night you can pump weights, dance Zumba, run the track, do downward dog and excel at the elliptical. It’s all happening at the ARC.

Take in a show

At the Mondavi Center for the Performing Arts, tickets for students are half off — and you get the first show free. Get ready to applaud.

Go clubbing

With more than 650 clubs, including Greek life, at UC Davis, you can lead, serve, learn and grow — and have a great time doing it.

Talk crafty to me

Express yourself at the Craft Center, which offers more than 90 classes each quarter, including ceramics, textiles, glass-blowing and welding.

Eat fresh and local

Take a basket and shop at our on-campus and city farmers markets for a variety of delicious and healthy food from our California Central Valley.

Do a cannon ball

When it’s hot, the place to go is the Rec Pool. You can chill out in the pool and — if you’re brave — jump off the high dive.

Join our communities

Become part of our Student Community Center, proud home of the Cross Cultural Center, LGBTQIARC and other dynamic student programs.

Compete for glory

Join one of 37 club sports teams with a chance to compete nationally. Or play for fun by joining one or more of our 27 intramural sports.

Paint the town blue and gold

Downtown Davis is steps off campus and waiting to entertain you with almost 100 restaurants, movie theaters, trivia nights, free concerts…

Escape on campus

If you need peace and quiet, stroll the arboretum. Check out exotic plants and enjoy ducks, squirrels and turtles. And, oh, otter sightings!

Go on an adventure

How about hiking, camping, backpacking, whitewater rafting or kayaking in the California wild and beyond? Check out Outdoor Adventures.
