By forging great friendships, joining clubs, playing sports and taking part in campus life, you will make the most of your time as a college student — and find out who you are. This campus is rich with traditions like Picnic Day and Homecoming, but you will also be part of our biggest Aggie tradition — doing things your way while transforming our university and making the world a better place. You’ll pick up life skills along the way, like leadership. And you’ll find UC Davis is one of a kind, like you.</p> </div> </div> <div class="mason-grid"> <div class="mason-grid__sizer"></div> <div class="mason-grid__gutter"></div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item"> <a href="#" class="marketing-highlight category-brand--california-poppy "> <div class="marketing-highlight__image"> <img src="../../images/sample/tiles/espn-basketball.jpg" alt="Enthusiastic basketball supporters" /> </div> <div class="marketing-highlight__body"> <h3 class="marketing-highlight__title category-brand__text ">Cheer your heart out</h3> <p>Our 23 Division I athletics teams love your support. Join Aggie Pack, the nation’s largest college spirit group, and show your Aggie spirit.</p> </div> </a> </div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item"> <a href="#" class="marketing-highlight category-brand--sunny-grass "> <div class="marketing-highlight__image"> <img src="../../images/sample/tiles/quad-group.jpg" alt="Socializing on the Quad" /> </div> <div class="marketing-highlight__body"> <h3 class="marketing-highlight__title category-brand__text ">Socialize</h3> <p>So many choices for meet ups: the Quad, the sports fields, the Silo, the arboretum, downtown, the CoHo … where will you start?</p> </div> </a> </div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item"> <a href="#" class="marketing-highlight category-brand--sky-blue "> <div class="marketing-highlight__image"> <img src="../../images/sample/tiles/arc-weightroom.jpg" alt="The weight room at the ARC" /> </div> <div class="marketing-highlight__body"> <h3 class="marketing-highlight__title category-brand__text ">Get moving</h3> <p>Day and night you can pump weights, dance Zumba, run the track, do downward dog and excel at the elliptical. You can chill out in the pool and — if you’re brave — jump off the high dive.</p> </div> </a> </div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item"> <a href="#" class="marketing-highlight category-brand--wine-grape "> <div class="marketing-highlight__image"> <img src="../../images/sample/tiles/community-training.jpg" alt="Trust training for interns" /> </div> <div class="marketing-highlight__body"> <h3 class="marketing-highlight__title category-brand__text ">Join our communities</h3> <p>Become part of our Student Community Center, proud home of the Cross Cultural Center, LGBTQIARC and other dynamic student programs.</p> </div> </a> </div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item"> <a href="#" class="marketing-highlight category-brand--western-redbud "> <div class="marketing-highlight__image"> <img src="../../images/sample/tiles/innertube-polo.jpg" alt="Innertube water polo game" /> </div> <div class="marketing-highlight__body"> <h3 class="marketing-highlight__title category-brand__text ">Compete for glory</h3> <p>Join one of 37 club sports teams with a chance to compete nationally. Or play for fun by joining one or more of our 27 intramural sports.</p> </div> </a> </div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item"> <a href="#" class="marketing-highlight category-brand--california-poppy "> <div class="marketing-highlight__image"> <img src="../../images/sample/tiles/art-hunt.jpg" alt="Public art in downtown Davis" /> </div> <div class="marketing-highlight__body"> <h3 class="marketing-highlight__title category-brand__text ">Paint the town blue and gold</h3> <p>Downtown Davis is steps off campus and waiting to entertain you with almost 100 restaurants, movie theaters, trivia nights, free concerts…</p> </div> </a> </div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item"> <a href="#" class="marketing-highlight category-brand--sunny-grass "> <div class="marketing-highlight__image"> <img src="../../images/sample/tiles/arboretum.jpg" alt="Taking a stroll through the Arboretum" /> </div> <div class="marketing-highlight__body"> <h3 class="marketing-highlight__title category-brand__text ">Escape on campus</h3> <p>If you need peace and quiet, stroll the arboretum. Check out exotic plants and enjoy ducks, squirrels and turtles. And, oh, otter sightings!</p> </div> </a> </div> <div class="panel-pane mason-grid__item"> <a href="#" class="marketing-highlight category-brand--sky-blue "> <div class="marketing-highlight__image"> <img src="../../images/sample/tiles/outdoor-adventures.jpg" alt="Kayaks galore at Outdoor Adventures" /> </div> <div class="marketing-highlight__body"> <h3 class="marketing-highlight__title category-brand__text ">Go on an adventure</h3> <p>How about hiking, camping, backpacking, whitewater rafting or kayaking in the California wild and beyond?
