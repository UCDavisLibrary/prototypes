At UC Davis research is in our DNA

Whether UC Davis is predicting the next global virus before it happens or developing more nutritious wheat for a hungry world, our research is making a world a better place to live. Here is where you will improve health, enrich life and help feed the world. Come to one of the top 10 public research universities to make your mark.

Undergrad opportunities

Put “research” on your résumé by attending UC Davis, where you get hands-on experience and the skills valued by employers.

Graduate programs

Get world-class mentoring and training from our faculty by choosing one of our 96 programs, many ranked top in the world.

Industry partnerships

If you are looking for a research university that nurtures a culture of innovation and cooperation, join us at UC Davis.
